About Us

A S Partners is one of the leading law firms of Delhi delivering the best legal and business solutions to its diverse clientele across a wide spectrum. Apart from providing a one-stop solution to all legal needs of our clients in Delhi-NCR, A S Partners has also a well-established practice in many states in India through its network of competent lawyers spread all across the country. A S Partners is probably the only law firm which tends to completely focus and eliminate the problems of its clients from their roots through micro and macro level analysis. A S Partners was established in Delhi and within a short duration it has become one of the leading law firms of Delhi which gets reflected in the clientele of the firm which includes, but not limited to, State Bank of India, Delhi Development Authority (DDA), RMB Financial Services, CL Products (India) Limited and Shri Lal Mahal Group of Companies, Pringle Homeware Pvt. Ltd., Contrans Cargo Pvt. Ltd., etc.

At A S Partners, we strive for providing a single window legal service provider in a dynamic commercial environment. We are known for our prowess in enabling our clients by helping them resolve their most demanding business challenges by providing practical and innovative advice. Through years of experience representing different organizations and institutions all over National Capital Region and beyond, our lawyers provide a unique understanding of all legal issues. Our approach is responsive, innovative and practical. The core philosophy of the firm remains client satisfaction beyond the best of our effort. The firm places a premium on transparency, insightfulness and high quality legal advice at competitive price. We at consider the practice of law to be, first and foremost, about our clients. We are resolute in our commitment to provide exceptional client service. We are recognized for the breadth and depth of the legal expertise and experience of our lawyers and we apply that expertise and experience to help our clients succeed in their legal matters. We do this by understanding our clients’ objectives, by developing a strong grasp of their business needs and their industry requirements and by building trusted relationships with their people.

A S PARTNERS believes in making long-lasting and committed relationships out of its clients without compromising even an iota on the professionalism. The trust garnered by a firm is the foundation of all relationships, legal services being no exception. Therefore, building strong working, committed & trusted relationships with its clients through transparency in its dealing is the cornerstone of the firm’s work ethos. Here, Client needs are properly understood and met in a professional, incisive and timely manner. The firm’s partners and associates across India provide legal advisory and expertise to its clients across diverse practice areas and industry verticals in the country. A S PARTNERS prides itself in having set an exemplary bar in client-attorney interaction through regular, lucid and comprehensive communication and providing customized legal opinion and advisory services, catering to each client's specific needs. The firm aspires to build long term relationship with its clients, through teamwork guided by integrity and principles of honesty, accountability and consistency. The firm understands the client perspective-the need for responsive legal advice, expertise, creativity and value.
